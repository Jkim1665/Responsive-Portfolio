About Jin

My name is Jin and I'm a Korean-American (2nd Gen) who loves sports, music, and programming?

To be honest, I don't know how I ended up here... and if I have any talent for this subject; one thing I do know is my work-ethic is aiight. My career trajectory changed in my early twenty's when I realized that, although I love helping people, I needed to help myself. I needed to master more than just the things I was comfortable with. One day, I met this programmer, at one of my old jobs, who told me to give this coding thing a try and that was the seed that got planted 2 years ago. I put that idea on the sidelines. I kept jumping from job to job to pay bills and along the way learned more about myself and developed my soft skills. I was bit of a brute. One memorable lesson I learned was in the kitchen. He said "many things can betray you (in life) but a knife and a fire never will." Needless to say, I experienced many cuts and burns.

The next eye-opening event was when I began to work at a catering job for a tech company. We made lunches for them everyday and was one of the reasons why I wanted to work at this specific job. One day when delivering food, I saw the work enviornment and thought to myself, damn these guys can drink and work?! Long story short, I met all types of people along the way who pushed me to this path and I'm so grateful for support. I don't need to be the best programmer in the class. I just want to be the best version of myself.
